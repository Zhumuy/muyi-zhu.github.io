Elder-friendly Transit Oriented Development

Adaptation and Renovation at Dong Baoxing Road Station

2022/12

Shanghai, China

Project Overview

To address the challenges faced by elderly individuals in transportation, we plan to comprehensively update and transform the Dongbaoxing Road subway station. Throughout this process, our priority will be to enhance pedestrian-friendly infrastructure, ensuring that seniors can easily and conveniently use public transportation. Simultaneously, we aim to improve outdated commercial formats to better meet the needs of the elderly. The plan includes introducing spaces tailored for elderly activities, such as leisure areas and reading corners, to enhance the station's comfort. Additionally, we will increase green spaces in the vicinity, providing a pleasant resting environment for seniors and promoting both physical and mental well-being.
