Urban Green Network Reconnection

San Francisco Regional Green Space System Planning and Street Renovation

2023/11

San Francisco, United States

Project Overview

Sustainable urban development must be built upon a healthier urban green space system. The planning of the green space system not only focuses on urban beautification but also emphasizes its ecological functions. Through an in-depth analysis of the greenery index of San Francisco's urban streets, we identified vulnerable areas within the city. By introducing innovative greening solutions such as rain gardens, we are committed to enhancing the resilience of the area, promoting ecological balance, and improving the overall quality of life for residents.
