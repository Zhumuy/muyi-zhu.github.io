Eco-Waterfront

South Boston Urban Design for Eco-friendly Commercial and Residential Districts

2023/05

Boston, United States

Project Overview

The construction of the ecological office area will become a leader in the development of the Boston South Bay area. This office area is not just a workplace but also a vibrant community. Its appeal extends beyond work needs, serving as a unique living space that provides residents with a close connection to nature. Talent from surrounding universities will find an ideal intersection of work and living here, forming a community with an innovative spirit and vitality. By creating a coastal ecological corridor and urban recreational greenways, the project will infuse the city with new energy while showcasing the concept of sustainable urban development.
